Our History



Making pizzas since 1953.


Founded in the early 1950s, the De Luca family created a unique pizza business along the Avenues in Salt Lake City, Utah; which is where the resturant got it's name. It is a place where customers tell us what they want, not what is only on the menu. This is the place where families, friends, individuals can come and order the best pizza in Salt Lake. Over the years we have adapted our recipes so anyone, including those with specific dietary needs, can still enjoy our pizzas just as the next person.




Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any vegan or vegetarian options?

Yes, we offer a vegan crust that is made with non-animal products, vegan cheese to put on the pizza, and vegetarian toppings.

Is there a gluten-free option?

Yes, we have a gluten-free crust that is made with special flour, along with our toppings that are all gluten-free as well.

Is it possible to eat at the pizzeria?

Yes, we do have a seating area if you chose to dine-in. We ask that you keep the limit to 6 people per table as our tables may not be able to hold everyone if more then that.

Do I need to make a reservation?

No, you are welcome to come in any time. However, there may be a slight waiting time depending on the time of day.

Can I get my pizza delivered?

Yes, although, there is a delivery fee and we can only deliver up to 15 miles from our business. We are hoping on expanding to have delivery stations throughout the Salt Lake Valley to allow easier delivery and pickup.

Where can I pick up my pizza?

Yes, you can pick up your pizza here at the pizzeria. We will notify you once you're pizza is made.

Can I order my pizza online?

Yes, we have updated our business to allow online orders along with in-person orders. We will notify you when we've recieved your order, and when your pizza is ready for pick-up. If you choose to deliver, we will notify you once we have arrived at your address of destination.
